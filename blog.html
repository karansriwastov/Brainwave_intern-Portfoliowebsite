Learning CLOUD COMPUTING

Cloud computing is the on-demand access of computing resources, including physical servers or virtual servers, data storage, networking capabilities, application development tools, software, AI-powered analytic tools, and more, over the internet with pay-per-use pricing. It offers greater flexibility and scalability compared to traditional on-premises infrastructure and has become indispensable in both personal and business settings
